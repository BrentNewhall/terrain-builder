How This Works

This app generates tabletop RPG terrain tiles and exports them for 3D printing.

Under the Design tab, use the Rows and Columns sliders to choose the size of the tile. Click the Walls buttons to add walls on any 4 sides.

Under the Customize tab, select a tile style and tap/click any tile to change it to that style. Ditto with the wall style. Click Add feature to add an object to the center of the tile.

Under the Print tab, click the Download button to download the entire tile as an STL file for 3D printing.

This app generates tiles that are 1.25" square and 0.25" walls. This allws a 1", 28mm-scale mini to fit on any square, even if that square contains walls, while also keeping all tiles a consistent size.
